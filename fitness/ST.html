Strength Training Workouts

Push-Up Training

Description: A full-body workout to strengthen your chest, shoulders, and triceps with push-ups.
Duration: 20 minutes.
Benefits: Builds upper body strength, improves core stability.
Key Muscles Targeted: Chest, shoulders, triceps, core.

Pull-Up Training

Description: A challenging bodyweight exercise to target the back and biceps.
Duration: 15 minutes.
Benefits: Strengthens upper back, biceps, and forearms.
Key Muscles Targeted: Back, biceps.

Dumbbell Bicep Curl

Description: A workout focusing on building arm strength, particularly for the biceps.
Duration: 15 minutes.
Benefits: Targets the biceps and forearms.
Key Muscles Targeted: Biceps, forearms.

Dumbbell Shoulder Press

Description: A great exercise to build strength in the shoulders and triceps.
Duration: 20 minutes.
Benefits: Strengthens shoulders, triceps, and upper chest.
Key Muscles Targeted: Shoulders, triceps.

Barbell Deadlift

Description: A strength-building exercise focusing on the back, hamstrings, and glutes.
Duration: 25 minutes.
Benefits: Builds lower back, glutes, and hamstrings strength.
Key Muscles Targeted: Hamstrings, glutes, lower back.

Barbell Squat

Description: A compound movement to strengthen the lower body, especially the quads and glutes.
Duration: 30 minutes.
Benefits: Builds lower body strength, improves core stability.
Key Muscles Targeted: Quads, hamstrings, glutes, core.

Kettlebell Swing

Description: A dynamic movement that targets the hips, glutes, and core.
Duration: 20 minutes.
Benefits: Improves posterior chain strength, core stability.
Key Muscles Targeted: Hips, glutes, lower back, core.

Goblet Squat

Description: A great exercise for targeting the legs and glutes with a kettlebell.
Duration: 25 minutes.
Benefits: Strengthens quads, glutes, and core.
Key Muscles Targeted: Quads, hamstrings, glutes, core.
