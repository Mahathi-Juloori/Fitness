YOGA WORKOUTS

Hatha Yoga Beginner

Description: A gentle introduction to Hatha yoga for beginners, focusing on foundational poses and breathing techniques.

Duration: 30 minutes

Benefits: Increases flexibility, improves posture, enhances relaxation, and promotes mental clarity.

Hatha Yoga Intermediate

Description: Intermediate Hatha yoga sequence to deepen flexibility and strength with more challenging postures.

Duration: 45 minutes

Benefits: Builds muscle strength, improves posture, enhances flexibility, and helps reduce stress.

Hatha Yoga Advanced

Description: Advanced Hatha yoga practice focusing on mastery of poses and breath control for enhanced mind-body connection.

Duration: 60 minutes

Benefits: Improves balance, builds strength, increases flexibility, and enhances mental clarity and focus.

Vinyasa Yoga Beginner

Description: A beginner-friendly Vinyasa flow focusing on smooth transitions between poses and developing a strong foundation.

Duration: 35 minutes

Benefits: Improves cardiovascular health, enhances flexibility, builds strength, and promotes mental focus.

Vinyasa Yoga Intermediate

Description: Intermediate Vinyasa flow emphasizing coordination between breath and movement, building strength and flexibility.

Duration: 50 minutes

Benefits: Increases strength, improves balance, enhances flexibility, and promotes relaxation.

Vinyasa Yoga Advanced

Description: Advanced Vinyasa yoga focusing on challenging poses, inversions, and intense breathing techniques for expert practitioners.

Duration: 60 minutes

Benefits: Builds strength, increases cardiovascular endurance, enhances flexibility, and promotes mental focus and clarity.
